Visualize your clients structure for tax planning

Clear picture of how assets and entities work together

Every accountant and tax attorney knows that the way clients organize their businesses and assets can have a big impact on their taxes. Main Street Pulse gives you and your clients a structure that simplifies tax planning. The software integrates all of the client's assets and business entities into one system, making it easy to see how the structure affects their tax strategy.

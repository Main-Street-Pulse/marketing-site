Bank-Level Security

We use end-to-end encryption to protect your data when it’s stored and when it’s being sent. Think of it like sealing your documents in a secure envelope—only you and the intended recipient can see what’s inside.

Built-In Defense

Our platform has built-in security features, including protection against common threats and vulnerabilities. We take extra steps to keep your information safe so you can focus on growing your investments with peace of mind.

Secure Connections

When you log in to Main Street Pulse, your connection is protected with HTTPS. This creates a secure line between your device and our platform, keeping your data safe from prying eyes.

Automatic Backups

We keep your data safe with automatic backups. This means that even if something unexpected happens, your important information is never lost.

We partner with Vercel to provide a platform that is not only secure but also fast and reliable. Vercel’s infrastructure is trusted by some of the biggest companies in the world, giving you confidence that your data is in good hands.
